**Subject:** Discrete Mathematics | **Course:** PCC-IT 401

### Q1. Propositional Logic
* **Sentence:** Declarative statement strictly True or False.
* **Conjunction ($p \land q$):** "AND" operator. True only if both are true.

### Q2. Equivalences and Tautologies
* **Tautology:** Compound proposition always True.
* $p \lor \sim p$: Law of Excluded Middle (Always True).

### Q3. Set Theory and Functions
* **Equivalence Relation:** Reflexive ($aRa$), Symmetric (if $aRb$ then $bRa$), Transitive.

### Q4. Posets and Lattices
* **Poset:** Reflexive, Antisymmetric, Transitive relation.
* **Lattice vs Poset:** A Lattice requires every pair to have a unique LUB and GLB. Not all posets have this, so not all are lattices.

### Q5. Group Theory
* **Group is Abelian iff $(a \circ b)^{-1} = a^{-1} \circ b^{-1}$:** Normal inverse rule is $b^{-1} \circ a^{-1}$. If abelian, this flips.
* **4th Roots of Unity:** $\{1, -1, i, -i\}$ forms a commutative group under multiplication.

### Q7. Graph Theory
* **Handshaking Theorem:** $\sum \text{deg}(v) = 2|E|$.
* **Isomorphism:** Bijective mapping preserving adjacency between two graphs.

### Q8. Graph Sub-types
* **Regular Graph:** Every vertex has same degree.
* **Bipartite Graph:** Vertices split into two disjoint sets; edges only cross between sets.
* **Chromatic Number of Tree:** 2.
