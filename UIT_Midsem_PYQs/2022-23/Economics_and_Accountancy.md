# B.E. 4th Semester Theoretical Examination, 2022-2023
**Subject:** Economics and Accountancy | **Course:** HSM-HU-401

### Q1. Economics and Markets
* **Micro vs Macro:** Micro studies individuals/firms (prices). Macro studies the entire economy (GDP, inflation).
* **Market Types:** Perfect Competition, Monopoly, Oligopoly, Monopolistic Competition.

### Q2. Demand and Elasticity
* **Demand Determinants:** Income, price of related goods, tastes, future expectations.
* **Elasticity:** Elastic (>1, luxury), Inelastic (<1, necessities).

### Q3. Revenue and Perfect Competition
* **AR vs MR:** Average Revenue is Total/Quantity. Marginal Revenue is extra revenue from one more unit. 
* In perfect competition, firms are price takers. Profit maximized where MR = MC.

### Q4. Production Functions
* **Law of Variable Proportions:** Adding variable inputs (labor) to fixed inputs (land) initially increases output rapidly, then slower, and eventually declines.

### Q6. Macroeconomic Variables and Accounting
* **Inflation:** Sustained increase in general price levels. Controlled by Central Bank interest rates.
* **Asset vs Liability:** Asset = owned value generating future benefit. Liability = debt owed to outsiders.

### Q7. Accounting Cycle
* Source Documents $\rightarrow$ Journalizing $\rightarrow$ Ledger $\rightarrow$ Trial Balance $\rightarrow$ Financial Statements (Income statement & Balance sheet).
