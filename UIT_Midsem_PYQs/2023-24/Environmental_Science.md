# B.E. Even Semester Midterm Examination, 2023-24(Even)
**Subject:** Environmental Science | **Code:** MC-HU 402

### Q1. Goal and applications of environmental engineering.
**Answer:** **Goal:** Ensure societal development aligns with nature. Protect humans from adverse environmental effects and protect the environment from human/industrial damage.
**Applications:** Water Supply/Treatment, Wastewater Management, Air Pollution Control, Solid Waste Management.

### Q2. Components of ecosystem and role of EIA.
**Answer:**
**Components:** Abiotic (non-living like soil, water) and Biotic (living like producers, consumers, decomposers).
**EIA Role:** Evaluates environmental impacts before projects begin. Proposes preventive measures, corrective actions, and compensatory measures for sustainable development.

### Q3. Explain nutrient cycle of an ecosystem.
**Answer:**
Nutrient cycle (biogeochemical cycle) is the pathway where vital elements (Carbon, Nitrogen) move through biotic and abiotic components. Producers absorb nutrients -> Consumers eat plants -> Decomposers break down dead matter -> Nutrients return to the soil/air.

### Q4. Define by-products. Sources, impact, control of soil pollution.
**Answer:**
**By-product:** A secondary, incidental product derived from a manufacturing process (e.g., molasses).
**Soil Pollution:** * Sources: Industrial waste, pesticides/fertilizers, solid waste.
* Impacts: Reduces fertility, contaminates groundwater, biomagnification.
* Control: Organic farming, effluent treatment, bioremediation.

### Q5. Describe energy flow model with laws of thermodynamics.
**Answer:**
Unidirectional flow governed by:
1. First Law (Conservation): Solar energy converts to chemical energy, transferred up the food chain. Total energy remains constant.
2. Second Law (Entropy): 10% Rule. Energy transfer is inefficient; ~90% lost as heat at each trophic level.
